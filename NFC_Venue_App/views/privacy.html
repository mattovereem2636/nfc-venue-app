What we collect

When you tap an NFC tag or visit this page, our web server records standard server log data: your IP address, browser type, device type, time of access, and the page requested. This data is used solely for security monitoring and system diagnostics.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information through this NFC system. We do not require you to create an account or log in.

Third-party services

This site may use a URL shortening service to route NFC tags. That service may independently log tap events including approximate location, device type, and access time. Please refer to your URL shortener's privacy policy for details.

We use Google Fonts to display typography. Google may collect standard font-request data per their privacy policy.

Cookies

This site uses a single session cookie for the administrator panel only. Guest-facing pages do not set any cookies.

Data retention

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days and then automatically deleted. No personal data is retained beyond this period.
